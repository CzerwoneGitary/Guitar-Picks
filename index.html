About Dunlop Tortex picks

The Tortex range has become the most well known of the Jim Dunlop plectrums...
So, while the shape may change, you'll always know which gauge to get by looking at the color of the Tortex pick.
Tortex picks range from a gauge of 0.50mm all the way up to 1.5mm.
